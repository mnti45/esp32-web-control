HUONG DAN KHONG CAN HOSTING

1. Giai nen file ZIP nay ra may tinh.
2. Bam dup vao file: BAT_DAU_MO_WEB_VA_TAO_QR.bat
3. Neu Windows Firewall hoi, bam Allow / Cho phep.
4. May tinh se tao file: QR_QUET_DE_VAO_WEB.png
5. Dien thoai ket noi cung Wi-Fi voi may tinh.
6. Quet ma QR do de vao web.

Luu y:
- Cach nay KHONG can upload hosting.
- Nhung chi dung duoc khi may tinh dang bat va dien thoai o cung mang Wi-Fi.
- Neu gui QR cho ban gai o xa, QR se khong mo duoc. Muon mo o xa bat buoc can hosting hoac public tunnel.
